Machine Learning

We set up an algorithm to systematically pick what college you should go to based on your game average statistics that we were going to include. during our testing and trying to make the model fit with a KNN model we were never able to get a reliable enough reading to say we could determine what school you should go to with any real accuracy. In fact it seemed as though the more trials we put our model through, the less accurate it got. Simply put, atheletes, or even people in general, are not easily quantified by a series of numbers.

 
 

Added Value Statistics

Added Value is a statistic given to ever player to help quanitify the impact they have on their team. Whether over their entire career, or just to the team they were drafted to, it seems even actual NFL analysts have a hard time giving precise estimations for how valuable a player can be. Although in comparison to all statistics it seems there will always be significant outliers in sports.
